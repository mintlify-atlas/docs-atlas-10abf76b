## Keyboard-first design

Every action has a keyboard shortcut. Press <kbd>/</kbd> at any time to see all shortcuts, or browse the [keyboard shortcuts reference](/reference/keyboard-shortcuts).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Visit [karan-gera.github.io/color-palette](https://karan-gera.github.io/color-palette/) — no install required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a palette">
    Press <kbd>R</kbd> to reroll colors, or <kbd>Q</kbd> to cycle through color relationship modes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock your favorites">
    Press <kbd>1</kbd>–<kbd>9</kbd> or <kbd>0</kbd> to lock individual colors, then reroll the rest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your palette">
    Press <kbd>E</kbd> to export to CSS, Tailwind, SCSS, JSON, or any design app format.
  </Step>
</Steps>
